Reverse Pac-Man
A strategic twist on the classic arcade formula inspired by Pac-Man but this time, you play as the ghost.
Instead of running from enemies, you hunt. Predict Pac-Man’s path, control the maze, and strike at the perfect moment.

Game OverView
Reverse Pac-Man flips the traditional maze-chase experience:
* You control a ghost
* Pac-Man is controlled by AI
* Your objective is to catch Pac-Man before he collects all pellets
* When Pac-Man eats a power pellet, the tables turn — you must survive until the effect ends

Core Mechanics
* Grid-based maze movement
* Pac-Man AI pathfinding
* Power pellet state switching
* Collision detection system
* Score & round tracking

Ghost Abilities
- Each round, the ghost may use special abilities:
* Phase Walk – Move through walls briefly
* Teleport – Warp to another maze location
* Fear Pulse – Temporarily slow Pac-Man
* Shadow Mode – Become invisible for a short time

Win Conditions
* Ghost Wins: Catch Pac-Man.
* Pac-Man Wins: Collect all pellets.
